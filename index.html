How to import a listing The tracker can't fetch job pages directly from your browser — most career sites block it. The most reliable workflow is:
  1. Paste the URL into chat with Claude and ask it to add the role to your tracker
  2. Or switch to Paste listing — copy all the text from the job page and drop it there
  3. Or use Find listing — type the role + company and Claude will search for a fetchable version
💡 For Ashby pages, the fetchable URL format is:
jobs.ashbyhq.com/[Company]/[job-id]
